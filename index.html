About

This game uses your webcam and hand tracking to control and throw a basketball. No installs needed—just open the page in a modern browser and allow camera access.

  • Move: your hand moves the ball (left-right is inverted).
  • Shoot: pinch thumb + index while moving your hand up.
  • Levels: higher levels have a moving rim.
