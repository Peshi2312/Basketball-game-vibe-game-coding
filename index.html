CHOOSE YOUR BALL

About Us

Welcome to AI HOOPS — a basketball game controlled by your hand in real-time. Just open the page, allow camera access, and start aiming.

How it works

We use your webcam and MediaPipe hand tracking to detect your hand position and pinch gesture. Your hand controls the ball on the court, and the pinch + upward flick releases the shot.

Controls

  • Move: move your hand to move the ball (left-right is inverted).
  • Shoot: pinch thumb + index, then flick your hand upward to throw.
  • Score: if the ball passes through the rim, you earn points.

Levels

Pick a difficulty to change the challenge. Higher levels make the rim move faster, so you need better timing and steadier throws.

How to play

1) Move the ball

Move your hand left or right to position the ball. (It is slightly inverted, so move opposite if needed.) Keep your hand in the camera view.

2) Aim with your pinch

Pinch your thumb + index when you are ready to shoot. Hold the pinch for a moment, then flick upward.

3) Score in the rim

When you release the ball, it will travel toward the hoop. If it passes through the rim, you score.

Quick tips

  • Flick upward faster for stronger shots.
  • Try shooting near the center of the rim opening.
  • For harder levels, the rim moves more—watch it and adjust.
